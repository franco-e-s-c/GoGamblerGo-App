Instructions

Pick one of the four decks (A, B, C, D) that are displayed by clicking over it.

Every deck that you pick has a posibility of winning or losing a specific amount of credits.

You may choose whichever deck you want at any moment of the test, even if you choose the same several times.

The goal of this test is to get as many credits as possible in the minimum time.

You will be given some credits as initial fund, your test will start once you click the button START

Good luck!
